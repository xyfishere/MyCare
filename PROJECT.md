# My Care

## Purpose

My Care is a gentle personal growth app for people who struggle with procrastination, task initiation, and maintaining routines under changing energy levels.

It helps users begin, focus, recover, and reflect without streak pressure or overwhelming checklists.

## Product Principles

- Progress without pressure.
- One clear next step at a time.
- Low-energy participation still counts.
- Private by default and local-first.
- Calm visuals support the experience rather than distract from it.
- Personalization should reduce friction, not add configuration burden.

## Core Product

- Morning and bedtime self-care routines.
- Focus sessions with breaks and category tracking.
- Goals, deadlines, completion check-ins, and optional notes.
- Habit Garden with low-energy alternatives.
- Private notes for the future self.
- Lightweight health, focus, habit, and goal statistics.
- Bilingual Chinese and English experience.
- Optional account login, cloud sync, history, and backup.

## Product Boundary

My Care is a self-management and reflection tool. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support.
